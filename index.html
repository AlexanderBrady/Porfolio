About me

My journey began in Architecture, leading to various roles such as Architectural designer and CAD designer. These roles honed my skills in design thinking and problem-solving. Currently, I am pursuing a Master's in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at the University of Nottingham, where I am expanding my expertise in user research and digital interface design. My unique background enables me to approach UX design with a holistic and user-centric perspective.
